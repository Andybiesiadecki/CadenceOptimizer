# STRDR - Complete Branding Guide

## 🚀 Brand Identity

### Brand Name
**STRDR** (pronounced "Strider")

### Tagline
**"CADENCE AND SPEED OPTIMIZER"**

### Brand Personality
- **Fast**: Lightning-quick performance optimization
- **Sleek**: Clean, minimalist design aesthetic  
- **Precise**: Data-driven, scientific approach
- **Professional**: Elite-level training tools
- **Accessible**: For runners of all levels

## 🎨 Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Pure White (#FFFFFF)
- **Secondary**: Deep Black (#000000)
- **Accent**: Light Gray (#F8F8F8) for subtle depth
- **Error/Alert**: Red (#FF3B30) for stop/warning states

### Typography
- **Style**: Bold, uppercase, increased letter spacing
- **Primary Font**: System fonts (SF Pro on iOS, Roboto on Android)
- **Weight**: 700-900 (Bold to Black)
- **Letter Spacing**: +1-2px for speed/precision feel

### Logo Concepts

#### Primary Concept: Lightning Bolt + Motion
```
⚡ STRDR
```
- Minimalist lightning bolt symbol
- Clean, bold typography
- High contrast black on white

#### Alternative Concepts:
1. **Stylized "S"** with speed lines
2. **Metronome pendulum** with lightning accent
3. **Circular cadence rings** around lightning bolt

## 📱 App Icon Design

### Primary Design (1024x1024)
- **Background**: Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- **Symbol**: Black lightning bolt in center
- **Motion Lines**: Subtle circular lines suggesting rhythm/cadence
- **Typography**: "STRDR" integrated or below symbol

### Technical Specifications
- **iOS**: 1024x1024px PNG (no transparency)
- **Android**: 512x512px + adaptive icon assets
- **Web**: 192x192px, 512x512px PWA icons
- **Formats**: PNG for production, SVG for source

- "Optimize your running with precision"

- Unrealistic promises

## 🏃‍♂️ Target Audience

### Primary Users
- **Serious Recreational Runners** (70%)
  - Training for marathons/half-marathons
  - Data-driven approach to improvement
  - Use GPS watches and fitness apps

- **Competitive Athletes** (20%)
  - Elite/sub-elite runners
  - Coaches and training groups
  - Performance optimization focus

- **Running Beginners** (10%)
  - Learning proper form
  - Seeking guidance and structure
  - Technology-comfortable

### User Personas

#### "Marathon Mike" (35, Software Engineer)
- Runs 40+ miles/week
- Owns Garmin watch, analyzes all data
- Goal: Sub-3:00 marathon
- Values: Precision, data, efficiency

#### "Coach Sarah" (42, Running Coach)
- Trains 20+ athletes
- Uses technology for group coaching
- Goal: Improve athlete performance
- Values: Professional tools, results

#### "Beginner Beth" (28, Marketing Manager)
- New to running, training for first 5K
- Overwhelmed by fitness apps
- Goal: Learn proper form, avoid injury
- Values: Guidance, simplicity, encouragement
